Quick start

  • Synthesize a partner:
    wendy net.owfn -o net.sa
  • Compute the characterization of all partners:
    wendy --og net.owfn -o net.og

FAQ

What are the system requirements for running Wendy?

Wendy runs on any architecture with a processor, some memory, and a supported operating system.

Why does a core dump occur every time I pick my nose?

Such actions sometimes interfere with core functions of the operation system. You might be interested in this particular solution.

My question was not answered by the FAQ, the documentation, and the manual. What can I do?

Please send your question to Wendy's maintainer.
